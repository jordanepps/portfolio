A little about me

Hi! I am Jordan, a developer native to New York City. Having grown up in one of the largest cities in the world for high-tech infrastructure, I was exposed to technology early in life. Curiosity quickly developed, especially pertaining to the internet. I wanted to know the science behind it all, so I taught myself how to code, and I have not stopped since.

I enjoy web development because it provides me the ability to manufacture anything I conceptualize. I am able to incorporate inspiration from the streets that catalyzed my interest for coding, which is my favorite part. There is always something new to learn, and there is always a more efficient way to produce the designs you desire. Web development consists of perpetual evolutions to the original structure, much like home in New York, and for that it will always be my passion.
