A little


about me

Hi! I am Jordan, a developer native to New York City. Having grown up in one of the largest cities in the world for high-tech infrastructure, I was exposed to technology early in life. Curiosity quickly developed, especially pertaining to the internet. I wanted to know the science behind it all, so I taught myself how to code, graduated from Thinkful's engingeering course, and I have not stopped since.

I enjoy web development because it provides me the ability to manufacture anything I conceptualize. I am able to incorporate inspiration from the streets that catalyzed my interest for coding, which is my favorite part. There is always something new to learn, and there is always a more efficient way to produce the designs you desire. Web development consists of perpetual evolutions to the original structure, much like home in New York, and for that it will always be my passion.

Find The Recipe

Submit an image that contains food and the Clarifai API will use artificial intelligence to determine possible matching ingredients. The ingredients are then sent to the Edamam API and recipes with ingredients most likely matching the image will be displayed in order. Register and log in to save your favorite recipes you find.

Made with: React NodeJSExpressPostgres
